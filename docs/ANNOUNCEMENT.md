## Hacker News — Show HN

**Title:** Show HN: iAny – Offline, on-device Khmer AI (RAG, STT, TTS, OCR), open source

> iAny is an offline-first Khmer AI app that runs entirely on the user's device.
> I built it to bring good AI to Khmer speakers without needing the cloud, an
> account, or a fast connection — and to grow open Khmer models that don't exist
> well yet.
>
> What it does:
> - RAG chat over your own docs, in Khmer or English, with cited sources. PGlite
>   (WASM Postgres) + pgvector in the browser; embeddings and a small LLM run via
>   Transformers.js / WebGPU. A React Native app does the same on-device with
>   op-sqlite, llama.rn and whisper.rn.
> - Open Khmer speech-to-text (Whisper fine-tune), text-to-speech (VITS), and OCR.
> - Accessibility: a Khmer Braille converter, and a sign-language data collector
>   that stores only MediaPipe hand landmarks — never video.
> - Community data drives (/voice, /scan, /sign) that build open CC-BY-SA-4.0
>   datasets, so the models keep improving.
>
> It's Apache-2.0. Backend is a Cloudflare Worker (model mirror, encrypted
> backups, the collectors). The business model is open code + hosted service /
> support / grants, not code secrecy.
>
> Try it: https://iany.app  ·  Code: https://github.com/sengtha/iAny
> Feedback very welcome — especially from Khmer speakers and on the on-device
> model quality.
