# iAny — open source & commercial model

iAny is **open source under Apache-2.0**. You are free to use, modify, and build
derivative products on top of it — including commercial ones — with no royalty
and no need to ask permission. That is intentional: the mission is to build the
best free, open Khmer AI (STT, TTS, OCR, sign language, and on-device RAG) **with
the community, for the community.**

This document explains how the project sustains itself *without* restricting the
code, so contributors and adopters know the deal.

## What is free and open

- **All the source code** in this repository (Apache-2.0).
- **The released Khmer models** (STT, TTS, OCR, and the sign-language model as it
  matures) — free to download and run on-device.
- **The community datasets** (`/voice`, `/scan`, `/sign`) — released openly
  (CC-BY-SA-4.0) with opt-in contributor credit.
- **Self-hosting** — you can deploy your own copy of the app and Worker.

## How iAny sustains itself

Revenue does **not** come from closing the code. It comes from things open source
doesn't give away for free:

1. **Building on it & support** — custom development, integration, training, and
   support for organizations that want to adopt or extend iAny.
2. **Hosted service & credits** — the convenience of not self-hosting: managed
   cloud sync/backup, hosted model mirror, larger cloud-assisted models, and
   higher usage tiers via a credits system. The code is open; paying for us to
   *run and scale it* is optional.
3. **B2B / B2G contracts** — bespoke Khmer AI (STT/TTS/OCR/sign) and deployments
   for telcos, banks, government, education, and accessibility organizations,
   with SLAs and support.
4. **Grants & donations** — funding for the public-good mission (Khmer language
   preservation, accessibility, digital inclusion).

Anyone is free to compete with the hosted service using this same code. Our moat
is not secrecy — it's the brand, the community and data flywheel, model quality,
reliability of the hosted service, and the expertise of the people who build it.
